About Us

Find&Dine is a mobile application which can detect the food dish by scanning a photo. It will scan the photo and detect the food name and displays the recipe (ingredients and steps to follow). Also, YouTube suggestion videos will be displayed on how to cook the detected food item. Find&Dine assures reliability and convenience and was born of the need to fulfill the recipe needs of food lovers. Find&Dine is a prototype of the Undergraduate Project developed for the Undergraduate Project of B.Sc.(Hons) Computer Science and Software Engineering - University of Bedfordshire - UK
